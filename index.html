ABOUT

Tadhana means fate in the Filipino language, a way in which through the years the members of this Los Angeles indie band came together. An odd combination of friends, musicians, sound manipulators and beyond, have combined female-driven harmonic vocals, indie pop influences, compelling guitarmonies and keys that lends a unique vibe to the local music scene.
